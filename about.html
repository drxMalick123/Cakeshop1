Our Story

Baking Happiness Since 2010

How It All Began

SweetBites Bakery started as a small home-based business in 2010, when our founder Sarah decided to turn her passion for baking into a profession. What began as baking cakes for friends and family quickly grew into a beloved local bakery.

After receiving countless requests for her delicious creations, Sarah opened our first physical location in 2015. Today, we've expanded to serve customers nationwide through our online store, but we've never lost sight of our roots - creating handcrafted cakes with love and the finest ingredients.

Every cake that leaves our kitchen is made with the same care and attention to detail as those first cakes Sarah baked in her home kitchen. We believe that special occasions deserve extraordinary desserts, and we're honored to be part of your celebrations.
